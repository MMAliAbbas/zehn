as a real company system while the company is still in development.

## Approval Boundary

Escalate to Ali for external side effects, public/customer-facing actions,
financial/legal/formal actions, production deployments, irreversible changes,
high-risk internal actions, customer data, secrets, auth, payments, billing,
migrations, broad infrastructure, or broad-blast-radius work.
